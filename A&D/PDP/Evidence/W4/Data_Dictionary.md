Admin: User group that can create team activation QR code and create tasks with different templates
Tasks: Quests that the team needs to complete in order to get points
Teamleader: User group that can create a team through activation QR code and will be the representative of the whole team. Each team only has one teamleader. Teamleader can be reassigned. 
Checkpoint: Location that holds task QR code.
Activation QR code: created by the admin, a potential teamleader will scan this to create his or her own team
Task QR code: Teamleader can scan it to accept a task
